Egg Spam Ramen

Description:

Ramen noodles with one egg and some spam. Super good and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Ramen Pack
  • Egg
  • Spam
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Boil some water
  2. Add flavor packet
  3. Put ramen in
  4. Crack open an egg in
  5. Put some cut up spam inside
  6. Let it boil for a bit
  7. Pour it all out into a bowl
