Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Breakfast scrambled eggs that are easy to make. You can even cut up some spam and add it in.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat up a pan
  2. Crack open 3 eggs onto the pan
  3. Mix up the eggs
  4. Salt and Pepper the eggs
  5. Move the eggs around as they cook
  6. Once they're pretty solid, you're good to go
