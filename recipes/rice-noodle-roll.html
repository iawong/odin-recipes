Rice Noodle Roll

Description:

Rice Noodle Rolls are mainly a Cantonese cuisine. There are many versions of it containing meat or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Lean Pork
  • Egg
  • Glutinous Rice Flour
  • Wheat Flour
  • Chinese Rice Flour

Steps:

  1. Stir the lean pork into mince, adding marinade to marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Cut the scallions into slices and crack several eggs, prepared as ingredients for use.
  3. Mix glutinous rice flour, Wheat flour, Chinese rice flour and water to form a paste.
  4. Brush the dish with a layer of vegetable oil, add minced meat, add a tablespoon of paste, and then add the scallion slices and the evenly beaten eggs.
  5. Boil the pot with water and put the dish on the steamer.
  6. Cover the lid and steam the dish of paste for about 3 minutes (open the lid, if you see the paste bubbling, it means the steaming is done).
  7. Fold the thin pancake into rice noodle rolls with a shovel.
